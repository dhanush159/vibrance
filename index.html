ABOUT VIBRANCE 2023

Vibrance is an annual event organized by the student council to bring together students, alumni, and the local community for a week of fun, music, and celebration.

As the month of February approaches, every BMSCE-ian unanimously awaits in eager anticipation of one event...one such extravaganza - Vibrance. Vibrance is an experience that mere words can't encapture. It is an ecstasy that demands to be tasted first-hand. Vibrance has been one of the flag-bearers of college fiestas in South India and has taken huge strides, ever since its inception in 1980, to attain its current prestige. This year marks our 43rd edition and Incident promises to be grander than ever before.
